What Are Reflection Tokens?

Reflection tokens (also called dividend tokens) charge a fee on every transaction — typically 3% to 6% — and redistribute that fee to all existing holders proportionally. This means you earn more tokens automatically every time someone buys or sells. No staking, no lockups, no complex DeFi strategies.
