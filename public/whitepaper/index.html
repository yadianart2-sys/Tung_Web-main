5 Passive Income — The Reflection Mechanism

TUNG uses a reflection (rebase) mechanism — not traditional staking. This means:

  • No lockups. Your tokens stay in your wallet. You can sell anytime.
  • No minimum. Hold $10 or $10,000 — you earn proportionally.
  • No action required. Rewards arrive automatically with every transaction on the network.
  • Compound effect. As trading volume grows, so do your rewards.

💰 Real Example

If you hold 1% of the TUNG supply and someone makes a $10,000 trade, the 3% dividend tax generates $300 in rewards. Of that, $3 goes directly to your wallet — instantly. No staking. No claiming. No gas fees to collect. It just appears.

6 Auto-Burn — Deflationary by Design

TUNG is a deflationary token. On every single transaction — buy or sell — a portion of the tax is sent directly to the dead wallet (0x0000...dead), permanently removing those tokens from circulation. They can never be recovered, sold, or transferred.

TUNG launched with 2,000,000 tokens pre-burned, signaling the project's commitment to a deflationary model from day one. With every trade, the circulating supply shrinks. Basic economics: less supply + growing demand = upward price pressure.
