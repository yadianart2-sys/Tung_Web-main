How to Buy TUNG Token

Follow this step-by-step guide to buy $TUNG on BNB Smart Chain and start earning 3% passive dividends automatically. Estimated time: 15 minutes.

Step 1

Install MetaMask Wallet

Download MetaMask from metamask.io (available as a browser extension for Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or as a mobile app). Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase.

Step 2

Add BNB Smart Chain Network

Open MetaMask, click the network dropdown, and select "Add Network". Enter:

  • Network Name: BNB Smart Chain
  • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed1.binance.org
  • Chain ID: 56
  • Symbol: BNB
  • Explorer: https://bscscan.com
Step 3

Buy BNB from an Exchange

Purchase BNB (Binance Coin) from Binance, KuCoin, Crypto.com, or Bybit. Withdraw the BNB to your MetaMask wallet address on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20). Make sure you have a small extra amount for gas fees (~$0.10 per transaction on BSC).

Step 4

Connect to Falco-X Swap

Visit swap.falcox.net and click "Connect Wallet". Select MetaMask and approve the connection. Falco-X Swap is the official DEX for the Falco-X ecosystem.

Step 5

Swap BNB for TUNG

On Falco-X Swap:

  1. Select "Swap" mode
  2. Choose BNB as the source token
  3. Paste the TUNG contract address as the destination token
  4. Set slippage to 6-8% (to account for the 6% transaction fee)
  5. Enter the amount of BNB you want to swap
  6. Click "Swap" and confirm the transaction in MetaMask

What Happens After You Buy?

Once you hold TUNG in your wallet, you automatically start earning 3% of every transaction as passive dividends. The tokens will accumulate in your wallet automatically. You can also stake your TUNG on Falco-X Swap for additional rewards.
